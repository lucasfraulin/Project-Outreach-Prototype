About Us

1993 was Project Outreach's first mission. We were invited to Danang Vietnam, by the local government and Danang General Hospital. Project Outreach was the first medical mission team to work in Danang after the end of the Vietnam war.

The mission focused on Reconstructive Surgery for cleft lip and palate. In 1995, and 1997, our mission expanded to include Pediatric Eye surgery, and Pediatric Urology. In 1998 Project Outreach brought a Pediatric Oncology team from Alberta Children's Hospital to work with our Vietnamese colleagues at the Ho Chi Minh Cancer Center.

Formal educational days were part of each mission.

In 1999 Project Outreach sponsored Pediatric nurses with subspecialities in Pediatric General Surgery and Oncology to spend a month at Alberta Children's Hospital. In 2000, with the support of the Alberta College of Physicians and Surgeons, Project Outreach sponsored Vietnamese physicians to complete a 3 month fellowship in Pediatric Anesthesia, General Surgery, Urology and Pediatric Oncology at the Alberta Children's Hospital.

Project Outreach's work continued from 2000 to 2005 with physicians and allied health care workers from Calgary, teaching at the National Institute of Pediatrics in Hanoi Vietnam.

​In 2008 Project Outreach was invited to Cuenca, Ecuador, to perform Pediatric Orthopedic surgery. In 2011 Project Outreach was invited by the health authority in Peru, to provide reconstructive Burn Surgery. We have had successful medical and surgical missions to Peru in 2011, 2012, 2013 and 2015. We are returning in 2019.

Project Outreach has remained close to our medical colleagues in Vietnam. In 2017 we returned to Vietnam, to help provide medical and surgical care to the children from the central highlands of Vietnam. We returned to Vietnam in 2018.
